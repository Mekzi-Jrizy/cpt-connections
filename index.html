How to use CPT Connections:

  • Click "Choose Files" or drag files to the upload area
  • Supported file types: images (JPG, PNG, GIF), audio (MP3, WAV), video (MP4, AVI), documents (PDF, DOC)
  • Files up to 1GB in size can be uploaded
  • Uploaded files are saved and available for download
  • When deployed to GitHub, others can download your uploaded files
